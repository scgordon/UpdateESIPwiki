==Dataset / Granule / Metadata Identifiers==
The importance of unique identifiers for data is well understood in terms of data citation and unambiguous attribution. As metadata are shared between National and International repositories it is becoming increasing important to be able to unambiguously identify and refer to specific records. This requirement is facilitated by including an identifier for metadata and data in the metadata. Some mechanism must exist for ensuring that these identifiers are unique. This is
         accomplished by specifying the naming authority or namespace for the identifier.
